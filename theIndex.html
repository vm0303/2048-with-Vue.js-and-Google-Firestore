2048 using Vue

Made by Vishal Madhav

Rules: Move the tiles using the arrow keys on the keyboard. Every time you move one, another tile should pop up in a random manner anywhere in the box. When two tiles with the same number on them collide with one another as you move them, they will merge into one tile with the sum of the numbers written on them initially. You win the game if the sum equals to 2048. Otherwise, you lose the game where there are no moves left on the board.

NOTE: If you can't move the tiles anymore with the arrow keys, then the game is automatically over. Whatever score you get below is your final.

Once you get that final score, enter your first name, the score you got, and the date you got that score (Example shown inside the text fields)
